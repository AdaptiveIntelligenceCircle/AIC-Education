# AIC-Education Code of Conduct

AIC-Education is committed to building a respectful, transparent, and responsible environment for collaboration.

---

## 1. Expected Behavior

### ✔ Intellectual Respect

* Debate ideas, not individuals.
* Support claims with reasoning and evidence.
* Accept constructive criticism.

### ✔ Ethical Responsibility

* Do not develop systems intended for manipulation or psychological exploitation.
* Avoid contributions that may create societal harm.
* Prioritize long-term well-being over short-term gains.

### ✔ Transparency

* Clearly document assumptions and limitations.
* Disclose conflicts of interest.
* Communicate uncertainty honestly.

---

## 2. Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Hate speech or exclusionary behavior.
* Use of the project for extremist political agendas.
* Development of deceptive or manipulative educational mechanisms.
* Intentional misinformation.

---

## 3. Enforcement

The AIC governance team reserves the right to:

* Issue warnings
* Temporarily suspend participation
* Remove contributors in cases of severe violations

All enforcement actions will aim to preserve fairness, proportionality, and transparency.
